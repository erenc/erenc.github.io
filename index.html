Red Hook Quantified Community

Quantifying a community is a crucial issue and it is important to measure it regarding to livable conditions of the society. This project aims to define and drive research of how micro-geography sensor data can be leveraged to explore neighborhood dynamics. In this perspective, Reed Hook Area is selected for implementing the idea of Quantifying Community with the sensors
